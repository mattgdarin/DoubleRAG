Acme Corp HR and Culture

Leadership Team
Sandra Okafor serves as CEO and oversees company strategy and investor relations.
James Whitfield is CTO and leads the R&D division in Austin.
Chief Revenue Officer Maria Delgado joined in 2021 and has overseen three consecutive years of double-digit revenue growth.
CFO Raymond Park joined from BlueWater Ventures in 2022, bringing financial discipline ahead of the Series C raise.

Employee Benefits
Acme Corp offers full medical, dental, and vision coverage for employees and dependents.
Employees receive 20 days of PTO annually, increasing to 25 days after five years of tenure.
Acme provides a 4% 401(k) match and an annual profit-sharing bonus tied to company performance.
Remote work is available for non-field roles, with a minimum of two in-office days per week for HQ staff.

Hiring and Culture
Acme prioritizes internal promotion — approximately 60% of senior roles in 2023 were filled internally.
The company runs a graduate engineering program, hiring 8-12 new engineers annually from universities in Texas, Colorado, and Ontario.
Acme was named one of Austin's Best Places to Work in 2022 and 2023 by the Austin Business Journal.
Voluntary attrition was 7% in 2023, below the industry average of 12%.

Training and Development
All field technicians complete a 6-week onboarding program covering safety, installation, and ClearFlow system operation.
Engineers have access to a $3,000 annual education budget for conferences, courses, and certifications.
Acme runs a mentorship program pairing junior engineers with senior staff for 12-month cycles.
